Booking Rules



  1. If you do not wish to travel, you must cancel your booking at least 3 hours prior to scheduled departure of the flight to be eligible for a refund. Refund will be made to you subject to applicable cancellation charges. Please note that cancellation policy is subject to change without notice.

  2. Passengers can book pre-paid excess baggage up to 1 hours prior to the scheduled departure of their flight via our website or call centre.

  3. Refund for cancelled tickets will be processed after the deduction of appropriate cancellation fees, which may take up to 7 working days.
